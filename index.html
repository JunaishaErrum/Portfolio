About me
UX · UI · Research · Design

From code to design, grounded in systems thinking

I’m Junaisha, a Product Designer based in Ranchi.

Most recently, I’ve worked with early-stage startups like Uzence and Wilson, where I contributed across research and design, building design systems from the ground up and improving AI-driven flows for a travel planning product. Working in fast-paced environments taught me how to balance business goals, navigate constraints, and stay focused on the “why” behind every decision.

I began my career in software engineering but gradually transitioned into UI/UX design. Over time, I realised I was more driven to solve problems by understanding user behaviour and everyday challenges, not just through code, but through thoughtful design. That technical foundation still shapes how I approach design today, helping me collaborate better and think in systems.

I’m naturally adaptable and comfortable navigating new challenges. Outside of work, I enjoy unwinding with a good psycho-thriller or rom-com movie, and exploring food, usually letting curiosity (and my nose) lead the way.
